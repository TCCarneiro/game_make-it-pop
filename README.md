🕹️ Survive the 9 to 5

A rhythm-based survival game about modern work chaos, burnout, and way too many Slack notifications.

📖 Overview

It’s Friday.
You’ve got a massive delivery due by EOD.
Slack won’t stop pinging.
Your burnout meter is creeping toward 100%.

One more notification… and it’s game over.

Survive the 9 to 5 is a small experimental rhythm game where players must dodge distractions and complete tasks under pressure, simulating the chaos of a real workday.

🎮 Gameplay
🧠 Manage Burnout
Keep your burnout meter under control or crash (literally).
💬 Dodge Notifications
Avoid incoming Slack messages trying to break your focus.
🖱️ Complete Tasks
Click and hold at the right timing to stay productive.
⚡ Rhythm-Based Interaction
Everything flows with timing and pressure.
🧪 Why This Project Exists

This project is an experiment in:

AI-assisted game development workflows
Rapid prototyping with minimal overhead
Blending 3D + AI-generated visuals
Exploring burnout and productivity through gameplay

Also… because Slack notifications are the real final boss.

🛠️ Built With
Claude (Anthropic) – Ideation, logic, and rapid prototyping
Higgsfield AI (Nano Banana Pro) – Visual style generation
Foundry Modo – Base 3D modeling (color-coded geo workflow)
Adobe Photoshop – Layer slicing (foreground/background compositing)
🎨 Art Workflow
Modeled simple geometry in Modo
Color-coded elements to describe scene logic
Used AI to generate stylized, toy-like visuals
Composited layers into foreground/background slices
Integrated into the game scene
⚙️ Tech Highlights
Mouse movement mapped to character arm
Simple but responsive input system
Lightweight architecture for fast iteration
Designed as a rapid prototype, not a full production game
