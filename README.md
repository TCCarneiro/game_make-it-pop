<h1 align="center">🕹️ Survive the 9 to 5</h1>

<p align="center">
  A rhythm-based survival game about modern work chaos, burnout, and way too many Slack notifications.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>📖 Overview</h2>

<p>
It’s Friday. You’ve got a massive delivery due by EOD. Slack won’t stop pinging. Your burnout meter is creeping toward 100%.
</p>

<p>
One more notification, and it’s game over.
</p>

<p>
<strong>Survive the 9 to 5</strong> is a small experimental rhythm game where players must dodge distractions and complete tasks under pressure, simulating the chaos of a real workday.
</p>

<h2>🎮 Gameplay</h2>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Manage Burnout</strong>, keep your burnout meter under control or crash, literally.</li>
  <li><strong>Dodge Notifications</strong>, avoid incoming Slack messages trying to break your focus.</li>
  <li><strong>Complete Tasks</strong>, click and hold at the right timing to stay productive.</li>
  <li><strong>Rhythm-Based Interaction</strong>, everything flows with timing and pressure.</li>
</ul>

<h2>🧪 Why This Project Exists</h2>

<p>
This project is an experiment in rapid game prototyping, AI-assisted development workflows, and building playful interactive experiences out of very real modern work anxiety.
</p>

<p>
It is also a creative exploration of how AI can help with ideation, visual development, and fast iteration across both web-based and game engine projects.
</p>

<p>
Also, Slack notifications are the real final boss.
</p>

<h2>🛠️ Built With</h2>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Anthropic Claude</strong>, ideation, logic, and rapid prototyping support</li>
  <li><strong>Higgsfield AI Nano Banana Pro</strong>, visual style generation</li>
  <li><strong>Foundry Modo</strong>, base 3D modeling and scene blockout</li>
  <li><strong>Adobe Photoshop</strong>, slicing foreground and background layers for composition</li>
</ul>

<h2>🎨 Art Workflow</h2>

<ol>
  <li>Modeled the scene in Foundry Modo using simple geometry.</li>
  <li>Color-coded the environment to define what each element represented.</li>
  <li>Used AI image generation to transform the blockout into a soft, cartoon, toy-like visual style.</li>
  <li>Masked and sliced the generated artwork in Photoshop.</li>
  <li>Separated scene elements into foreground and background layers for depth and presentation.</li>
  <li>Integrated everything into the game prototype.</li>
</ol>

<h2>⚙️ Features</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Rhythm-inspired survival gameplay</li>
  <li>Burnout meter as a fail-state mechanic</li>
  <li>Task completion through click-and-hold interactions</li>
  <li>Mouse movement mapped to the character’s right arm</li>
  <li>Fast, lightweight prototype structure</li>
</ul>
